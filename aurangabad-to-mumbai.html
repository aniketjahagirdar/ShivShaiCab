The distance between Aurangabad and Mumbai is 335 kilometers (208 miles). The travel time by car is about 6 hours and 30 minutes. There are a few different ways to get from Aurangabad to Mumbai:If you have more money and you want to save time, you can travel by taxi or flight. Taxi travel is the most expensive, but it is also the most convenient. Flight travel is the fastest way to travel, but it is also the most expensive. The best time to travel from Aurangabad to Mumbai is between October and March. During this time, the weather is pleasant and there is less chance of rain.
